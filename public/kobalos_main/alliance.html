VALHALLA
This Guild is one of the largest networks of top-ranking, respectable MuTizens in Bahr headed by GM [DL]MERLIN. Named after the Norse God Odin's battle angels who were sent to our world's battlefields to escort fallen heroes to the God's immortal halls, VALHALLA Guild never fails to show moral support to its allies and deadly grace to its challengers.


ARRIBA
Another one of Bahr server's foremost respectable Guilds known for its camaraderie and unity, this Guild headed by GM [DL]xdude was named after its members' Alma Matter.


NETORUIS
The legendary Guild NETORIOUS traces its origins way back since Mu Philippine's first patch, under the leadership of Bahr's yet another excellent GM as legendary as the guild itself: [DL]Netori. Now headed by GM filyoung, NETORIOUS Guild lives up to its name as one of the hottest but comfortable Guilds home to Bahr's finest warriors.


PUNISHER
One of the top guilds in Bahr in terms of camaraderie, unity and friendship, Guild Punisher never fails to uphold the virtues of a true player: deadly in battle and honorable in dealings.
