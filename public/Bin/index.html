Origin of Guild name:
The name KoB/\Los is the greek word for goblin. History: We were once members of the Netorius Alliance; known for its supreme power and fair dealings. The guild reigned and dominated the Bhar server for years. Unfortunately, due to some unforeseen events, our guild master [DL]Netori quit the alliance and left everything and everyone behind. It was all a matter of one’s beliefs and principles. Then the members came up with the idea of reguilding which led to the birth of a new guild; KoB/\Los.

ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS:

What does it feel like to be chosen guild of the month? It was overwhelming and we feel thrilled and honoured that we were chosen as the guild of the month. This definitely proves that other players and game masters acknowledge our abilities and that we can be an inspiration if not a model to other guilds for them to carry on and be the best that they can be. 		Beliefs/tenets: The guild believes and practices “oneness and fair dealing”. We play fair and achieve our goals with decorum and honor. We keep our principles intact no matter what happens.
