Do you have a home server? Bhar server 1

What are the qualifications to join the guild? Is there a screening process? The player must be at least level 320, with good set, weapon/s, wings and must have been referred to us by our members.


Any particular event KoB/\Los is most active in?
KoB/\Los guild is active in the Castle Seige and MU Liga events, especially the Kill Schriker Event where we established the record for 16 Schrikers killed in 5 minutes for both Titan and Bhar servers

What makes KoB/\Los stand out from other guilds? Killing Kundun in all 4 servers of Bhar daily (at times twice a day) with just 1 party makes KoB/\Los stand out from other guilds. Aside from that, we also have the past and current Duel Masters and loyal members united as one, building and sharing a strong and harmonious relationship among themselves.

Any thoughts you'd like to share for start-up guilds? Trust and loyalty should be practiced to have a stronger relationship among the members of the guild. It is very important to have an active Guild Master to administer his/her members properly. It is also important to have mutual respect and understanding to avoid miscommunications and misunderstandings in order to reach the ideal guild we all want to have. 		Where do you see KoB/\Los going in, say, the next couple of years? Seeing us grow stronger and more active is our goal in the near future to add more actions and thrills here in Bhar server.

Any specific hangout or meeting place?
Since KoB/\los guild is still new in the MU society, we haven't discussed where to hold EBs or hangouts yet. Maybe we will talk about that soon.
